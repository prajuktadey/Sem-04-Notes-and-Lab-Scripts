Welcome to Amazon!

Amazon.com, Inc. is an American multinational technology company focusing on e-commerce, cloud computing, online advertising, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence.


Amazon was founded by Jeff Bezos from his garage in Bellevue, Washington,[7] on July 5, 1994.


Initially an online marketplace for books, it has expanded into a multitude of product categories, a strategy that has earned it the moniker The Everything Store.
